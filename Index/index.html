About Me

I am Andrea Gail A. Evangelio. I was born on November 27, 2001, in Quezon City. Currently, I live in Malabon City. I am the second child among my other three siblings. At home, I love to annoy my family.

After my first year of college in nursing at Manila Central University, I decided to shift to Industrial Design at the University of the Philippines-Diliman. However, I failed the Talent Determination Test. Now, I am taking a Bachelor of Science in Information Technology at St. Paul University- Quezon City. I never thought I would be taking BSIT in college, as the careers I wanted were in the branches of medicine, visual arts, writing, and humanities. Here I am now in the field of technology, where I try to figure out everything.

Ever since I was a child, I already had the hunger to give life to my ideas. It started from drawing to exploring different mediums like embroidery, clay sculpting, and beading. While I used to think that being an artist was not a practical career, I am now creating accessories for different individuals to earn money. My other hobbies are journaling, playing games, and falling in love with fictional men. When I can, I would like to smash the patriarchy.

Senior High School Pharos Volume 2 Issue 1

Senior High School Pharos is the official news publication for the Senior High School Department of Manila Central University.

Read the full issue here!

Republic Act No.11053: Anti-Hazing Act of 2018

With multiple organizations in Manila Central University, the Visual Arts Guild of the Creative Arts Group created a video, which features Gylfie, to remind the Centralinos of the Republic Act No. 11053, known as the Anti-Hazing Act of 2018.

For this video, I did all of the illustrations in IbisPaint X. I edited the video in collaboration with my other member in Visual Arts Guild in Filmora.
